FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS!

For all clients the spray tans can last 4-7 days. Daily activities, skin type, and post-tan mantinence can change the duration.

-Shave at least 8 hours before. Any waxing, pedicures, and manicures should be done at least 24 hours before.

-Exfoliate your skin up to 24 hours prior to being sprayed. For ideal exfoliation/skin prep, you may purchase an Exmitt at the time of your session for $6.

-Have your skin free of all lotion, makeup, deodorant, and perfume. Anything that creates a barrier on your skin will alter the effectiveness of the spray.

-Wear loose fitting clothing and shoes It is key that you do not get wet, sweat or shower for 8-10 hours after your spray to allow your color to set properly. After the minimum time has lapsed, you may shower, avoiding any harsh scrubbing.
